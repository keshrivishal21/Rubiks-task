Design Team of the Rubiks Club: Shaping Visual Identity and Creativity The Design Team of a college club is responsible for crafting the visual identity of the club and communicating its message through creative and engaging designs. Whether it's creating promotional materials, digital content, or branding assets, the design team ensures that the club's presence is visually appealing and aligns with its goals. This team is essential in making the club’s activities, events, and initiatives stand out, both online and offline. Objectives of the Design Team Branding and Visual Identity: The design team works to create a cohesive brand identity for the club, including designing logos, color schemes, typography, and other visual elements that reflect the club’s values and personality. Event Promotion: They are responsible for designing posters, banners, social media graphics, and flyers to promote upcoming events, workshops, and activities organized by the club. Digital Content Creation: The team designs digital content, including graphics for social media posts, website elements, newsletters, and email campaigns, ensuring that the club's digital presence is vibrant and engaging. Creative Projects: Beyond regular club activities, the design team also participates in creative initiatives such as merchandise design (T-shirts, mugs), yearbooks, event photo albums, and visual storytelling.
